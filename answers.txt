Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Both allow for you to integrate changes between branches, but in the case of merge, the entire history is preserved in the log, with branches not being changed, while with rebase the entire new branch is added onto main with new commits for each in the branch. This creates a more linear history in the log.
